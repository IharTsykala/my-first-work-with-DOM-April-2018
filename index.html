Static data base for days of the week

If you want to see the next day, click “Next” or press “=>” on the keyboard.

If you want to see the previous day, click “Prev” or press “<=” on the keyboard.

If you want to close the window notification without changes, click “X” or press “Esc” on the keyboard.

If you want to show information about your selected day, click “Enter” or press “Enter” on the keyboard.
